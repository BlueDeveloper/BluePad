Response Time

Most tickets are answered within 1–2 business days (KST, GMT+9). Bug reports and refund requests are prioritized. We do not currently offer phone support.

Refund Policy

BluePad Pro is a digital product. The following terms apply:

  • 14-day window — refund requests are accepted within 14 days of purchase, no questions asked.
  • Activation does not void refund — you can try the full Pro features and still request a refund within the window.
  • License deactivation — once a refund is approved, the license key is permanently disabled on all your devices.
  • How to request — select "Refund Request" in the form, include your email and license key. We process the refund through Paddle (the payment processor) within 1–2 business days.

Lost License Key

If you lost your license key, use the form with "Other" type and include the email used at purchase. We will look it up and resend within 1–2 business days. Maximum 3 devices per key — if you need to move to a new computer, use the "Deactivate" button in the app's License dialog first.

Trial Questions

The 14-day trial unlocks all Pro features without signup. If your trial expired and you forgot to save your work, simply renew using "Other" type in the form — we extend trials on a case-by-case basis for genuine users.

Bug Reports

Please include: OS version, BluePad version (Settings > About), steps to reproduce, and any error messages. Screenshots help a lot. Critical bugs (data loss, crashes) are usually patched within a few days.
